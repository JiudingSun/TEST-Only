OFF THE
BEATEN PATH
‘Off the Beaten Path’, essentially means “a place where few people go, far from any main roads and towns”. The road less traveled is sometimes fraught with barricades bumps and uncharted terrain. But it is on that road where your character is truly tested and have the courage to accept that you’re not perfect, nothing is and no one is — and that’s OK.
